Ad Adcta Adsumus is latin for We are the records. In this song I wanted to depict the chaos of humanity through remixing several commercials. This song tells the story of accidents, car insurance, kakis, and the many side effects that come from life. I have always wanted to learn to mix and create music, so I had a lot of fun with this assignment. The album cover was generated in Midjourney. I then hand drew the fire cycle animation in Adobe Animate. The album cover is supposed to represent the begginng and the end of humanity with an added dash of car insurance.
