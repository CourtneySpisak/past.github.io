12 Months of Bad CGI Sharks

Premier Pro

GarageBand

Dalle2

When tasked with making a video depicting twelve months of something, the first subject that come to my mind was bad CGI sharks. As a fan of Syfy shark movies, I thought that this would be a strange and querky video. Also I knew that there were at least twelve horrible shark movies to choose from. I compiled all of the clips in Premier Pro, along with an extended version of the Sharknado theme. I extended the Sharknado theme in Garageband. The thumbnail was made in Dalle2.
