Postcard Project

Photoshop

The Postcard Project consists of a series of three postcards representing the state of Wyoming each in a different style. I chose Wyoming randomly. I have no personal connection to this state. The first postcard is probably the most viusally complex. The background is an image of Yellowstone National Park. Within the text which reads, "Wyoming", a series of landmarks significant to the state are depicted. The second postcard leans into the cowboy aspect of the state. This is a much more simple design with a cowboy walking accros a simple geometric background. The third postcard depicts the sign for Yellowstone National Park. This post card only utulizes seven colors and very minumal details.
