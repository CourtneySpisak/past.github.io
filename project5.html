Design a New Species

Photoshop

The little fellow below is Nacho the Happy Abomination. He is a new species constructed from the face of a quokka, the body of a cheetah, the front feet of an eagle, the back feet of a frog, and the antlers of an elk. He was assembled in Photoshop.
