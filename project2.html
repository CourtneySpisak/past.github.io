Pattern Design

Illustrator

The images below are patterns that I designed in Adobe Illustrator. The way that these patterns were constructed was that I loosely traced images with the pen tool. When tracing the images, I added and subtracted different elements to give the traced image a different feel from the original. The first pattern is created of repeating gemoetric flowers. When constructing this pattern, I was inspired by the geometric shapes that are often found in my beadwork patterns. In the end when this pattern was mirrored and repeated resembled a wild garden made of gemoetric succulents. The second image is a pattern of a funky pigeon illustration.
