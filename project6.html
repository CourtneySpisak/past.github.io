Design a Font

Photoshop

The Twenty-six Letters of Squidward is a font made of Squidward. This is probably one of the pieces that I am most proud of from this class for it took a lot of time and patience to find and contruct each of these letters. In order to make the letters, I when frame by frame through several episodes of Spongebob searching for Squidward poses that resemble the specific letter. Some of the letters were practically impossble to find, so I extended the font to Squidward and objects relating to Squidward.
